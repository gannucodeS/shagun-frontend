✦ Our Story ✦

Where Tradition Meets Timeless Elegance

Founded in the heart of Ajmer, Shagun Wedding Planners has spent over fifteen years weaving the rich tapestry of Rajasthani culture into celebrations that families cherish for generations. Every ceremony we orchestrate is a testament to our devotion to beauty, precision, and love.

From the sacred ghats of Pushkar to the majestic havelis of the Pink City, we bring your vision to life with the grandeur it deserves.
